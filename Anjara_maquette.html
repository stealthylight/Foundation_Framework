Guides

  • Preparations before Cooking
    Hygiene

    Before Cooking, One should always do some checkings in different aspects. One of the most important to check is cleanliness.

    Make sure that every materials are clean, ingredients are still fresh as to not affecting negatively the health of those who'll eat the food.

    Be detailed and See the End

    Prepare every ingredients beforehand and read carefully each step of the process.

    This readiness will smooth your cooking as a butter leading possibly to a qualitative increase of the resulting product

    Be always prepared before cooking.

  • Random Tips

    If you are a novice chef, Consider to keep a timer near. Timing is a hard thing for beginner to grasp and it is often a barrier to become a better chief.

    Maintain a moderate and reasonable ratio between ingredients and seasonning so to keep the food's quality.
